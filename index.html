Overview

Multiverz

The overview of this Multiverz project is to generate a product that can act as a guide to a job seeker on a particluar selected domain by providing available job oppurtunities depending on their current skills set. Additionally, it provides valuable information about the skills sets and the courses available to acquire them for any chosen domain by the job seeker.

As a part of Job Aggregators team, we were assigned to scrape one million unique job postings and structure them as per defined unified schema in the span of 8 sprints.


About Web scraping


Initial thoughts on web scraping as a work would be trivial, easy, and unimportant. However, I have realised through the 4 weeks of internship at Multiverse that it is one of the most important steps in the Data science pipeline which most of us turn a blind eye to.

Structured data is of paramount importance when it comes to data analytics but more than 70% of data present online is unstructured and hence is of little use. However, Web scraping allows us to “legally” collect information from thousands of websites and structure the data into desirable format which eases further steps of the Data science pipeline.



About Scrapy tool


There exists a number of ways and libraries to perform the task of web scraping. I have chosen the scrapy tool to do so as it is very fast and supports scalability on an enterprise level. Working of the scrapy tool can be summarized as follows.



Sprint 1


As a part of sprint 1, I started researching how to use a scrapy tool and scraped all the available Data Scientist job postings from Indeed India website. Structured the obtained data into the required pre-defined schema as a JSON format. Converted it into a CSV file.

Sprint 2


For the sprint 2, researched on SkillsFuture Singapore website and fetched the data. Figured out the architecture of SkillsFuture website where competency levels for both the technical and generic skills for each job role is given. Obtained the required skills competency levels data for each job role under different sectors and structured it into CSV file.

Sprint 3


As a part of sprint 3, Scraped all the available job postings from the Exxon Mobile which stands 3rd in Fortune 500 (2020) companies. Additionally fetched data for all the job postings using Scrapy tool from Indeed USA website. As the data is in structured format, scraping these job aggregator websites are much easier than company websites. On a whole scraped 2K unique job postings and processed the obtained data into predefined schema and structured it into a CSV file.

Sprint 4


For the sprint 4, Scraped all the available job postings from two of the top 20 Fortune 500 companies (2020) which are AT&T and Walgreens websites using Scrapy tool. On a whole scraped 13K unique job postings and processed the obtained data into predefined schema and structured it into a CSV file.

Sprint 5


As a part of sprint 5, Scraped all the available job postings from four of the top 100 Fortune 500 companies (2020) which are Kroger, HCA Healthcare, Intel and AlbertSons websites using Scrapy as well as JSON and Selenium tools. On a whole scraped 16K unique job postings and processed the obtained data into predefined schema and structured it into a CSV file.

Sprint 6


For the sprint 6, Scraped all the available job postings from four of the Fortune 500 companies (2020) which are Cognizant, DaVita, Jabil and ADP websites using Scrapy and Selenium tools. On a whole scraped 24K unique job postings and processed the obtained data into predefined schema and structured it into a CSV file.

Sprint 7


As a part of sprint 7, Scraped all the available job postings from Whatjobs aggregator website using Scrapy tool. On a whole scraped 91K unique job postings and processed the obtained data into predefined schema and structured it into a CSV file.

Sprint 8


For the sprint 8 to achieve scraping of 1 million unique job postings, scraped available job postings from Mashable and Jobisjob aggregator websites using Scrapy tool. On a whole scraped 250K unique job postings and processed the obtained data into predefined schema and structured it into a CSV file.
